# Executive Summary

Lawable is a legal career acceleration platform designed to bridge the gap between legal education and legal employment.

The platform helps law students gain practical exposure through internships, mentorship, legal projects, skill development resources, and networking opportunities with legal professionals.

The first version of Lawable focuses exclusively on becoming the default professional development platform for law students.

The platform does NOT initially provide direct legal services to clients, litigation support, compliance services, or lawyer marketplaces.

The MVP objective is to build a trusted ecosystem of law students, mentors, legal professionals, recruiters, and organizations.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Lawable becomes India's largest legal professional ecosystem connecting students, lawyers, recruiters, educators, compliance professionals, and legal technology tools through a unified platform.

Students enter through career development.

Organizations enter through hiring.

Mentors enter through networking.

The network effect creates a scalable legal ecosystem that later expands into education, legal services, compliance solutions, and AI-powered legal products.
